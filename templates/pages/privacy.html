Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the absolute minimum information required to provide our service. When you upload a file, we temporarily store it on our secure servers solely for the purpose of processing your request.

2. Document Storage and Auto-Deletion

Your privacy is our highest priority. All uploaded and converted files are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers 1 hour after processing (or 24 hours if you are a registered user saving to your dashboard). We do not keep backups of your files.

3. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep track of your session and conversion history so we can display them on your dashboard. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or sell your browsing data.

4. Security Measures

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you submit or access your documents, including HTTPS encryption (TLS) on all data transfers.
